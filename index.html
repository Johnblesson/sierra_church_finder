How To Use This Application

  • This application is very easy to use. Go to the Get Started or Church Finder. It will take you to a unique interface.

  • You can search by location and church name. We recommend you search by location. When you search by location or area, you eventually see all the churches in that area.

  • If you know the name of the church you when attend, just search for the name of the church, and all the keyword of what you typed will be listed.

  • After successful search you will see the name the church, address, location/area and most importantly an active contact to call for direction.

  • When you finally see the church you want attend, copy the address and the location and paste it on the map for google map direction.
